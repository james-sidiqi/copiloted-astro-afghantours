# AfghanTours — Contact Instructions (Strict Enforcement)

## Purpose
Defines the contact page and inquiry conversion flow.

---

## Non-Negotiable Rules
- The page must support high-intent inquiries.
- The inquiry form must be easy to find.
- Social links must not overpower the inquiry flow.
- The layout must remain clean and premium.

---

## Required Contact Page Structure
1. Intro
2. Contact methods
3. Inquiry form
4. WhatsApp / social
5. Optional office reference
6. Optional response expectations

---

## Inquiry Form
Preferred fields:
- name
- email
- WhatsApp
- destination interest
- dates
- group size
- message

Optional:
- preferred tier
- Dubai escort interest

---

## Contact Methods
Support:
- email
- WhatsApp
- inquiry form
- social media

---

## Tone
Direct, helpful, professional.

---

## Visual Goal
The page should feel:
- clear
- premium
- low-friction
- trustworthy
