# AfghanTours — About Instructions (Strict Enforcement)

## Purpose
Defines how the About page and related trust / policy pages should work.

---

## Non-Negotiable Rules
- The About page must build trust.
- It must feel grounded and credible.
- It must not sound like generic corporate filler.
- It must not make exaggerated claims.

---

## Required About Page Structure
1. Company introduction
2. Leadership
3. Operating philosophy
4. Why travel with AfghanTours
5. Safety and planning approach
6. Social impact / responsible tourism
7. Licensing / credibility
8. Related FAQs or policy links

---

## Company Introduction
Must clearly explain:
- who AfghanTours is
- what the company does
- why it exists

---

## Leadership
Leadership should be presented clearly and credibly.
Avoid padded bios.

---

## Operating Philosophy
Explain:
- how AfghanTours thinks about route planning
- how the company balances realism and premium service
- why the company uses its hub system

---

## Why Travel With AfghanTours
This section should emphasize:
- local expertise
- planning discipline
- route realism
- operational capability
- trust

---

## Safety / Planning
This section must feel serious and measured.
Do not overpromise.
Do not imply impossible guarantees.

---

## Social Impact
If included:
- keep it real
- keep it modest
- make it concrete

---

## Tone
Professional, calm, informed, grounded.
