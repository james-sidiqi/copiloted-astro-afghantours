# AfghanTours — Dishes Instructions (Strict Enforcement)

## Purpose
Defines how dishes and food content should work across the site.

Food supports:
- cultural depth
- hospitality branding
- page richness
- SEO
- destination storytelling

---

## Non-Negotiable Rules
- Keep food content authentic.
- Keep it concise.
- Keep it culturally grounded.
- Use strong real photography.
- Do not let dishes pages become over-stylized or fluffy.

---

## Main Dishes Page
Should:
- feel warm and visually rich
- support browsing by region or category if relevant
- stay easy to scan

Each card should include:
- image
- title
- short description
- regional context if relevant

---

## Dish Detail Page Structure
1. Title
2. Main image
3. Regional context
4. Short intro
5. Cultural explanation
6. Category / meal context
7. When travelers may encounter it
8. Related regions or tours

---

## Content Rules
Use:
- direct, warm language
- meaningful cultural context
- regional grounding where useful

Avoid:
- overblown restaurant language
- generic food-blog filler
- invented historical claims

---

## Visual Goal
Pages should feel:
- authentic
- appetizing
- warm
- elegant
